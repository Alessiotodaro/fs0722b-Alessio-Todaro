Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie.

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions,tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Images: Sally Vegan

Fresh Cancrberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegear
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 onces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, tough it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough
    2. In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix toggether all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed.
